\begin{frame}{Integrated Takeaways}
  \begin{enumerate}
    \item Model quality is necessary but insufficient: production success depends on deployment and monitoring discipline.
    \item Explainability is part of product quality, not a post-launch add-on.
    \item Forecasting and sequential decision systems require explicit handling of temporal structure and drift.
    \item Strong teams combine statistical rigor, engineering practices, and domain context.
  \end{enumerate}
\end{frame}
